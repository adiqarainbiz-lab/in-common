## Full Description (Google Play + App Store)

**Support local. Earn rewards. Build community.**

In Common is the loyalty app for Jerusalem's Palestinian-owned businesses. Every time you visit a partner café, shop, or service, you earn Common Points — redeemable as store credit on your next visit.

**How it works:**
• Sign up with your phone number — no bank account needed
• Show your QR code at any partner business when you pay
• Earn points instantly, tracked automatically
• Redeem points as store credit on future visits
• Unlock exclusive member deals and offers

**Tiers & rewards:**
As your points grow, you rise through membership tiers — Seedling, Olive, Cedar, and Keffiyeh — each unlocking better perks and recognition.

**Your community, your app:**
In Common was built to strengthen the local economy of Jerusalem. Every purchase at a partner business keeps money circulating in the community. The more you shop local, the more you earn — and the stronger the community grows.

**Features:**
✓ Instant QR-code check-in — no card, no cash required
✓ Real-time points balance and transaction history
✓ Exclusive deals and time-limited offers from partner businesses
✓ Referral rewards — invite friends, both of you earn bonus points
✓ 11 achievement badges to unlock
✓ Browse all partner businesses by category
✓ Push notifications for new offers (optional)

Join In Common. Shop local. Earn together.
